About Me

Building at the intersection of sound & sight.

I'm a creative technologist driven by curiosity — whether that means crafting synth textures in Logic Pro, generating living visual art in code, or diving deep into how systems work and why civilizations rise and fall.

My work lives where disciplines collide. I'm equally at home writing code and sculpting sound, looking for the moments where technology becomes art and art becomes experience.

When I'm not producing or building, you'll find me reading about Byzantine history, exploring new AI tools, or wrangling rogue squirrels in elf costumes.
